Buddha - Dharma - Sangha

Who we are?

The Sub30s Buddhist Group is a place for younger people to get together regularly to practice meditation and study Buddhism.

What we do?

Practice

Every meet up we practice either Meta Bhavana and the Mindfulness of Breathing meditations in a group environment - see the 'Buddhism and Meditations' page for more information about both meditations.

Study

Buddhist texts are taught, studied and discussed within like-minded groups of people who share relatable experiences of being spiritually disconnected within a modern western world.

Socialize

A fun and friendly environment with many planned social events.
